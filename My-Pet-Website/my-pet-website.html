Moments in the life of Zinder

From the view of her litter box custodian

Zinder is a wild Lynx Point who was found by her caretaker whilst working on a farm. He brought her home after harvest to save her from freezing during the Winter.

Here are some activities Zinder enjoys

  • Gazing into the horizon from the porch
  • Laying majestically around the house, switching locations often
  • Being scratched behind her ears and under her chin
  • Doing backflips in attempt to catch dandelion pollen

Here are some things Zinder does not enjoy one bit

  • An imperfectly clean water dish
  • Seeing any portion of the bottom of the food dish
  • New people and animals
